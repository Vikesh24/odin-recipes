Coconut Milk Ice Cream

Home

Description

This coconut milk ice cream is a rich and creamy dairy-free dessert that's perfect for those who are lactose intolerant or following a vegan diet. Made with full-fat coconut milk, it has a natural sweetness and a subtle coconut flavor that pairs well with a variety of toppings and mix-ins.

Ingredients

  • 1 (13.5 ounce) can unsweetened coconut milk
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Whisk together coconut milk, sugar, vanilla extract, and salt in a small bowl until sugar dissolves. Chill until cold, at least 2 hours. Churn in an ice cream maker according to manufacturer's directions. Freeze in an airtight container at least 4 hours or up to 3 months.
  2. Enjoy!
